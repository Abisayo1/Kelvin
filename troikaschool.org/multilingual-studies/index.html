Multilingual Studies

About Our Curriculum

In 2013, we included Mandarin (Chinese) to our curriculum

The inclusion of Mandarin (Chinese) in our Curriculum since 2013 in addition to French is to further give our pupils the competitive edge required for business in the 21st century in line with our motto; Value-added Advantage.

China is not just the worlds oldest and richest culture (over 5000 years old), it is the most populous nation in the world, with 1.28 billion people.

One fifth of the planet speaks Chinese and Mandarin Chinese is the mother tongue of over 873 million people, making it the most widely spoken first language in the world.

With Chinas ever growing economy and dominance, Troika Schools multilingual studies prepare children for the future that is already here.
